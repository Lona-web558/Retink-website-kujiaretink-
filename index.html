About Us

Who we are?

KujiaRetink Resellers programme is a digital product and technology mentorship programme
by KujiaRetink Network that gives marginalized and vulnerable youths access to create financial
stability for themselves by selling digital products and services.

Why we help?

The problem

In Kenya, marginalized youths, vulnerable young women, refugees, youths in poverty and youths
with disabilities, face multiple challenges that hinder their socio-economic development. Access
to technology that enables individuals to learn digital skills and earn money are limited for them.
This has created a digital divide, limiting them from improving their lives and communities.

Our Solution

KujiaRetink is a mentorship programme that seeks to end poverty by creating jobs, leveraging
the digital economy by mentoring marginalized youths in Kenya to earn a living, start and run
their own digital marketing or sales agency, and employ others to sustain the system.
